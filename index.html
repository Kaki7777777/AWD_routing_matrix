üéØ Origin AWD Best-Fit Criteria

Use Origin AWD When:

  • TAIL velocity + SKU-Heavy: Consolidation reduces per-SKU handling costs
  • Arbitrage sellers: Need flexibility to shift inventory between regions based on profitability
  • Uncertain demand patterns: Want to delay regional commitment until demand signals emerge
  • Cross-border compliance: Need staging area for regulatory processing before regional distribution

Skip Origin AWD When:

  • HEAD/BODY velocity: Speed to market more important than consolidation savings
  • Predictable regional demand: Can ship directly to known destination markets
  • Single-market sellers: No need for cross-region flexibility
  • High-value/time-sensitive: Minimize handling points

‚ö° Direct to AFN Criteria

Go Direct to AFN When:

  • Ultra-fast velocity + Predictable demand: No benefit from AWD buffering, direct flow needed
  • Time-sensitive products: Product launches, trending items, flash sales
  • Single AFN FC destination: When seller knows exact FC target (local/regional sellers)
  • High handling sensitivity: Fragile, perishable, or high-value items
  • Established direct relationships: Sellers with existing AFN FC contracts

Use Destination AWD When:

  • Multi-FC distribution needed: Inventory needs to spread across multiple AFN FCs
  • Demand uncertainty: Don't know which specific FCs will need inventory
  • Seasonal buffering: Need staging before peak season FC capacity constraints
  • Cross-docking benefits: Consolidation with other shipments reduces costs
